Cross River State

Cross River State, located in the South-South region of Nigeria, is known for its rich cultural heritage, lush tropical landscapes, and vibrant tourism industry. The state is home to the famous Obudu Mountain Resort, the Cross River National Park, and the annual Calabar Carnival, often called “Africa’s biggest street party.” With its diverse ethnic groups and hospitable people, Cross River State is a hub of natural beauty, history, and cultural celebration.
